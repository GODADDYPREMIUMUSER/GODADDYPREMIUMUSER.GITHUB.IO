 The very foundations of this institution are driven by the power and synergy of these two words combined Virtue and Knowledge . Virtue and Knowledge combined is greater than the total power achieved by each working separately. Knowledge alone is not enough,knowledge plus virtue is the true goal of an educational system. We at Mary Mount believe that knowledge gives you power but virtue respect.

In the year 2010, Mary Mount School began its journey in pursuit of meaningful education. Today, the school has evolved into an institution that delivers its promise of virtue and knowledge and is still growing. Dolly's Educational Trust is the parent trust that manages the Mary Mount School. The trust aims at providing valuable life long learning experiences to all its students. The trust believes an investment in knowledge pays the best interest. MARY MOUNT PRIMARY SCHOOL is an English medium, with play group, Nursery to Standard V. Mary Mount Primary School (MMPS) has been well thought of both in its philosophy and methodology to bring about a radical change and stimulating growth and development to a child's linguistic, physical, socio-emotional, cognitive and techniques. The aim of this institution is to impart to its pupils a sound, moral, intellectual and skill based education with a view to prepare them notonly with accumulated knowledge but also the accumulated experience of social living and enlightened leadership. Today, when there is cut-throat competition, massive challenges and ever changing scenarios, we at MMPS strive to work towards shaping a confident and befitting generation.
