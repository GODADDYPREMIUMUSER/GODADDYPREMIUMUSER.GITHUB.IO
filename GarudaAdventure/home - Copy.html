Paramotering

Paramotor is consists of a frame that combines the motor, propeller, harness (with integrated seat) and cage. We are using internationalized standard Paramoter with high power.

Paraglyding

Paragliding is the recreational and competitive adventure sport of flying paragliders. We are using lightweight, free-flying, foot-launched glider with super high speed.

woki toki

A walkie-talkie a more formally known as a handheld transceiver is a portable, two-way radio transceiver. We are using this equipment for flying pilot and customer safty.

Helmets

A helmet is a form of protective gear worn to protect the head from injuries. We are using international standard US maded high quality techinical helmets with wireless communication.

parachute

A parachute is a slow the motion of an object through an atmosphere by creating drag device. We are using parachute in spare for safty,save life and avoid any techinical circumstance.
